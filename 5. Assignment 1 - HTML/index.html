Our travel agency, myTravel, offers the most affordable 7-day and 6-night tour of Singapore. Singapore is a top travel destination, known for its stunning attractions and cultural diversity.

It is an island country and city-state in Southeast Asia. The country's territory includes one main island, 63 satellite islands and islets, and one outlying islet — making it a fascinating place to explore. Singapore's tropical climate is warm and pleasant year-round, making it ideal for tourists. What makes Singapore unique is its perfect blend of modern architecture, lush greenery, diverse cultures, and world-class cleanliness — all packed into one vibrant city.

Tourist Attractions in Singapore

1. Marina Bay Sands

One of the most iconic landmarks in Singapore, Marina Bay Sands offers a world-class hotel, a massive infinity pool on the rooftop, and a skypark with breathtaking views of the city.

  • Unique rooftop infinity pool experience
  • Light and water show every night
  • Premium shopping and fine dining options

2.Gardens by the Bay

A futuristic nature park with stunning Supertree structures, biodomes, and beautiful light displays at night. Perfect for nature lovers and photographers.

  • Cloud Forest with indoor waterfall
  • Supertree Grove light and sound show
  • Eco-friendly and educational experience

Sentosa Island

A popular island resort filled with attractions like Universal Studios Singapore, Adventure Cove Waterpark, beaches, and luxury resorts.

  • Ideal for family fun and adventures.
  • Indoor Skydiving and Zipline adventures.
  • Easy access by a capable car or monorail.
